Data Deletion Instructions

Sen App — How to delete your data

How to Delete Your Data

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request complete deletion of all data associated with your account using any of the methods below.

Option 1: Email Request

  1. Send an email to brianmuluvi@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the Instagram username associated with your account.
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Option 2: Revoke Access via Instagram

  1. Open Instagram and go to your Profile.
  2. Tap Settings > Security > Apps and Websites.
  3. Find "Sen" in the list of active apps.
  4. Tap Remove to revoke the app's access to your account.
What gets deleted: All personal data, Instagram account data, analytics data, scheduled content, and any other information associated with your account in our systems.

Data Deletion Timeline

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  1. Acknowledge your request within 48 hours.
  2. Complete deletion of all your data within 30 days.
  3. Send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Some data may be retained if required by law, but only for the minimum period necessary.
