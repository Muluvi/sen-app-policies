Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sen ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Instagram

When you connect your Instagram account to our App, we may access the following data through the Instagram API, with your explicit permission:

  • Profile Information: Your Instagram username, profile picture, bio, and account type.
  • Media Content: Your posts, stories, reels, images, videos, captions, and associated metadata (such as timestamps and location tags you have made public).
  • Insights & Analytics: Account engagement metrics, follower demographics, reach, impressions, and content performance data.
  • Publishing Data: Content you choose to schedule or publish through our App.

2.2 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account registration details (name, email address).
  • Preferences and settings you configure within the App.
  • Communications you send to us (support inquiries, feedback).

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers).
  • Usage data (features accessed, time spent, interaction patterns).
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, access times).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your Instagram content within the App.
  • Provide analytics and insights about your Instagram account performance.
  • Schedule and publish content to your Instagram account on your behalf.
  • Improve and personalize your experience with the App.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or Instagram data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that assist us in operating the App (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your data.

5. Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, secure server infrastructure, and regular security assessments. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Instagram data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8). Upon account deletion or data deletion request, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with Instagram (Meta Platforms, Inc.). Your use of Instagram is governed by Meta's own privacy policy and terms of service. We encourage you to review Meta's privacy practices at Instagram's Privacy Policy.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and Instagram data from our systems.
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your Instagram account from the App at any time through your Instagram settings under Apps and Websites.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

9. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data:

  • Email us at the address below with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  • Revoke the App's access through your Instagram account settings (Settings > Security > Apps and Websites).

We will process deletion requests within 30 days and confirm completion via email.

10. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
